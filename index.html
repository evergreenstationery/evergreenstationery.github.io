About Us

Evergreen Stationery Merchant Nigeria Limited is a leading stationery outfit in the South West of Nigeria, dedicated to providing reliable and high-quality stationery solutions for offices, schools, institutions, and professionals. The company is owned and led by Rotarian Ajibade Dauda Kolawole, the Chief Executive Officer, whose entrepreneurial vision and commitment to excellence continue to drive the growth of the business.

We deal in all kinds of quality stationery, including office equipment, architectural materials, laminating materials, calculators, notebooks, and a wide range of other essential stationery items. Our focus on quality products, dependable service, and customer satisfaction has earned us a strong reputation as a trusted partner in stationery supply across the region.

At Evergreen Stationery Merchant, we are committed to supporting productivity, creativity, and efficiency through durable products and professional service.

Founder & Visionary

Rotarian Ajibade
Dauda Kolawole PHF

Rotarian Ajibade Dauda Kolawole was born into the family of Mr. & Mrs. Ajibade Akinade on the 17th of July, 1969 in Ode-Omu in Ayedade Local Government Area of Osun State..

He had his elementary education in St. Mary’s Catholic primary school Ilesha, Osun State between 1976 – 1982, where he obtained his primary school leaving certificate. He later proceeded to St. Lawrence’s grammar school Ilesha for his secondary education between 1982 – 1987.

The quest for higher education saw him through to Ogun State University now (OOU) where he bagged a Bachelor degree in Religious Studies with a second class upper division in 1998.

He furthered his post graduate studies to the University of Ibadan where he obtained a Masters Degree in Guidance and Counselling Education in the year 2001.

Rotarian Ajibade Dauda Kolawole observed a compulsory one year National Youth Service scheme in Delta State. And his primary assignment was undertaken at Aghaga Comprehensive College, Oghara between April 19th, 1999 and February 2000. In his days at undergraduate level, he was a member of MAN O’WAR club of Nigeria, Ogun State University Unit where he rose to become Deputy Chief Co-ordinator in 1998. He was a participant on course No: (SS.324) Citizenship and Leadership Training Centre, Shere hills, Jos, Nigeria in 1997. He was also a participant on course No: (SH.400) Citizenship and Leadership Training Centre, sea school, Apapa Lagos, Nigeria in 1998.

He is a recipient of a prestigious’ Gold Statemanship Award of MAN O’WAR Nigeria. He is an honourary member of 32 Artillery brigade. He is currently the public relations officer of the honourary mess. He is a member of Police Community Relations Committee Zone 17 and currently the financial secretary. Rotarian Ajibade Dauda Kolawole has since forayed into trading and today he is the Chief Executive Officer of EVERGREEN STATIONERY MERCHANT NIGERIA LIMITED: a leading stationery outfit in South West of Nigeria.

He is happily married and blessed with blessed children.
